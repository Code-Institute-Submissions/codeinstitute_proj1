Inkwell Shards

Dublin literary club, opened to all new, talented and interested writers living in the Dublin area. We accept submission of all kinds, from poetry, to drama, to TikToks. Meeting monthly, in person and on , via Zoom!

Annual Irish Writer's Gala | 21.08.2088
Gaiety Theatre, Dublin

This event is for you! We have a limited number of places for this Sunday's Wicklow Walk. You need a moderate level of fitness, hiking boots, rain gear, water and a picnic lunch. We meet at the Roundwood Inn, then carpool and to Lough Dan and then head out on the Wicklow Way for a relaxed walk in with your fellow hikers. After a while we stop for a picnic lunch and then return back to the Roundwood Inn for coffee/tea and scones and for those rugby enthusiats you are welcome to watch Ireland V England.
